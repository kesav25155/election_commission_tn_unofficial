FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What are the eligibility criteria for voter registration?
A: To be eligible for voter registration, an individual must be a citizen of India and at least 18 years old as of January 1 of the year in which the electoral roll is being revised.
Q: How can I check my voter registration status online?
A: You can check your voter registration status online by visiting the official website of the Election Commission and using the voter status check tool. Enter your details to view your current registration status.
Q: What documents are required for voter registration?
A: The documents required for voter registration include proof of identity (such as Aadhar card, passport, or driver's license) and proof of residence (such as utility bills or rent agreement).
Q: How can I find my polling booth location?
A: You can find your polling booth location by checking your voter ID card or by using the online polling booth locator tool provided by the Election Commission. Enter your details to get information about your assigned polling booth.
Q: What should I do if I find an error in my voter ID card?
A: If you find an error in your voter ID card, you should contact the local election office immediately. They will provide guidance on the necessary steps to correct the error, which may include submitting a correction form along with supporting documents.
