Election Commission of Tamil Nadu

About Us

Welcome to the official website of the Election Commission of Tamil Nadu, where we strive to uphold democratic values by ensuring free, fair, and transparent elections that reflect the will of the people.

Vision Statement

"To foster a vibrant and participatory democracy by conducting elections with integrity, impartiality, and accessibility, thereby empowering citizens to exercise their right to vote and shape the future of Tamil Nadu."

Key Responsibilities

Conducting Elections

We are entrusted with the responsibility of conducting various elections, including Legislative Assembly elections, Lok Sabha elections, and local body elections.

Voter Education and Awareness

Promoting voter education is a core aspect of our mission. We work towards enhancing awareness among voters about the importance of participation in the electoral process.

Electoral Roll Management

Maintaining accurate and up-to-date electoral rolls is crucial for the integrity of the electoral process. We focus on continuous efforts to streamline the voter registration process and ensure the completeness of electoral rolls.

Implementation of Electoral Laws

As custodians of the democratic process, we enforce electoral laws to create a level playing field for all political parties and candidates.
